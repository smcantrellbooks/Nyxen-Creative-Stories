Getting Started

Welcome to Nyxen Creative Stories! Here is how to get the most out of the platform:

1. Set up your story — Use the Story Setup panel in Nyxen to choose your genre, tone, and audience.

2. Chat with Nyxen — Ask Nyxen to help you write stories, chapters, scenes, dialogue, or poetry.

3. Create visuals — Use Visual Studio to generate images and videos for your stories.

4. Publish when ready — Move your finished work to S.M. Cantrell Books for publishing.

Your chat history is saved per session. Log in to keep your conversations across visits.
